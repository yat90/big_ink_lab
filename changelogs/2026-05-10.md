## [Unreleased]

### Added (web)

- **Tablet breakpoint (`apps/web/src/styles/tokens.css`, `layout.css`):** Introduced a `768px` breakpoint. Spacing tokens `--space-md/lg/xl` scale up at tablet. `.main` gets wider top/side padding and drops the mobile-nav bottom clearance. `.topbar` gets more horizontal padding and gap. `.page-title` scales from `1.5rem` → `1.875rem` and `.page-sub` from `0.9375rem` → `1rem` for improved mid-width readability.

### Fixed (web)

- **Viewport (`apps/web/src/app.html`):** Removed `maximum-scale=1` and `user-scalable=no` from the viewport meta tag, restoring pinch-to-zoom for low-vision users and assistive workflows. Double-tap zoom is already suppressed via `touch-action: manipulation` on `html` in `apps/web/src/styles/layout.css`.
